about us!

 

Welcome to Matchalicious, where we proudly blend tradition and innovation to create exceptional matcha experiences. As a Filipino-owned business, we take immense pride in infusing our rich cultural heritage into every cup. Our journey begins with the finest matcha, sourced for its quality and authenticity, which we then craft into delightful beverages with a touch of Filipino flair. From the vibrant Strawberry Matcha Latte, beloved by college students in the Philippines, to our unique Ube Latte that celebrates the fusion of Filipino and Japanese flavors, every drink we serve is a testament to our passion for quality and creativity. We are dedicated to offering you not just a drink, but a moment of joy and connection. At Matchalicious, we invite you to experience the heart of our Filipino roots and the elegance of traditional matcha, all while savoring the delightful flavors that make our offerings truly special. Join us in celebrating the beauty of cultural fusion and the pleasure of exceptional beverages crafted with love.

our specialties!

classic matcha latte

Simplicity at its finest—our Classic Matcha Latte is a rich, creamy blend of premium matcha and velvety milk, delivering a smooth and earthy taste in every sip. Perfect for matcha lovers and first-timers alike!

strawberry matcha latte

S $2.99 | M $3.99 | L $4.99

Experience a delightful fusion of flavors with our Strawberry Matcha Latte—a beloved favorite among college students in the Philippines. We blend rich, premium matcha with the sweet essence of ripe strawberries, creating a creamy, refreshing drink that is both invigorating and satisfying. Perfect for a mid-study boost or a relaxing treat, this vibrant latte brings a touch of elegance and joy to every sip.

ube matcha latte

S $2.99 | M $3.99 | L $4.99

Indulge in the harmonious blend of cultures with our Ube Latte—a delightful fusion of Filipino and Japanese flavors. This unique latte combines the rich, creamy taste of ube, a beloved Filipino purple yam, with the smooth, earthy notes of premium matcha. The result is a vibrant, flavorful drink that celebrates the best of both worlds, offering a taste of tradition with a modern twist. Perfect for those who appreciate a global flavor journey in every sip!
