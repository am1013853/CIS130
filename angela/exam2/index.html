About Us!

At Family Pet Resort, we are an all-inclusive all-suites resort that provides everything for your pet during their stay with us. We are happy to provide every guest with plush bedding and plenty of toys, so there is no need to bring any from home. We proudly serve Canidae brand Low Residue dog food; however, if you choose to provide your own food, please provide pre-measured baggies with feeding instructions. For those dogs with a sweet tooth, ice cream treats and biscuits are available, as well as gourmet stuffed kong treats.
